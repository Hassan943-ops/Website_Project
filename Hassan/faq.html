How do I find someone to do a job?

As a Service Seeker, navigate to Academic Jobs/Other Jobs on your Home screen. Fill out the form and send proposals to providers you think are suitable for your task.

How do I start a job after I've sent the proposal?

After sending proposals, providers are notified. Once they agree, you'll see those proposals in Pending Proposals. Accept the proposal you prefer to start the job.

How many providers can I send proposals to?

You can send proposals to as many providers as you like. We recommend reviewing portfolios and profiles to ensure chosen providers are qualified for your task.

How do I set a price for the task?

ARTFORGE offers two pricing options: Fixed and Hourly. For one-time submissions, we recommend Fixed pricing. For ongoing tasks, choose Hourly

How do I complete or end a job?

Once the provider notifies you of task completion: 1. Go to the Ongoing Job 2. Click Complete to view the provider's bank details 3. Transfer the fee 4. Click Confirm The job will be marked as completed once the provider receives payment.

When do I pay the provider?

Payment is typically made at the end of a job. For longer jobs, we recommend regular communication with the provider.

How do I receive payment as a provider?

You'll receive payment in the bank account specified in your profile's Bank Details. Notify the seeker once you've completed the task. Do not mark the job as complete until you've received payment.

Is there a platform fee?

ARTFORG charges a 10% fee on your total monthly earnings. This fee supports platform improvements and service quality.

How do I post my skills?

As a Service Provider: 1. Click "Offer Skill" on your Home screen 2. Fill out the details 3. Pay special attention to pricing

Can I display my previous projects and experience?

Yes, you can showcase previous projects in your Portfolio on your Profile screen. Experience details are entered once during signup.

How can I stand out as a provider?

1. Showcase your best work in your portfolio 2. Set competitive prices based on current market rates 3. Provide detailed descriptions of your offered skills

What kind of skills can I post?

ARTFORGE welcomes a diverse range of skills, from everyday tasks to specialized industry skills. You can offer any skill you possess to start earning.

As a Seeker

Contact providers through the Chat button on their profile or the Job Details screen.

As a Provider

Wait until a job has started, then use the in-platform chat to communicate with the seeker.

Can I use an external platform for communication?

We strongly recommend against using external platforms. ARTFORGE is not responsible for issues arising outside the platform. For safety and security, please use only ARTFORGE's communication tools.

Why does ARTFORGE collect personal data?

ARTFORGE collects personal data to: 1. Ensure the safety of all platform users 2. Maintain high-quality services for both Service Seekers and Service Providers
